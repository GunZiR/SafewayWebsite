About US

2004

{% blocktrans %} Safeway Tailgate Co., Ltd. was established on January 8, 2004 with an initial registered capital of 5 million Baht. Initially, the company's headquarters was located in Nong Ree Subdistrict, Mueang District, Chonburi Province, with an area of 800 square meters, and have been audited the registration be progressed to the quality assurance standard ISO9001 : 2000. {% endblocktrans %}

2013

{% blocktrans %} The company has changed its headquarters to Nong Chak Subdistrict, Ban Bung District, Chonburi Province, with an area of 11,200 square meters. The registered capital has also been increased to 20 million Baht. {% endblocktrans %}

2014

{% blocktrans %} We have started expanding our logistics channels by exporting our products to various countries such as Cambodia, Malaysia, Laos, Myanmar, Vietnam, Indonesia, and Taiwan. {% endblocktrans %}

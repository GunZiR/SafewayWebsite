After Sales Service

Exclusively for products purchased from Safeway Tailgate Co., Ltd. If the product experiences any abnormal functioning or defects, we will offer a warranty coverage for a period of 2 years. This warranty applies to products specifically from Safeway Tailgate Co., Ltd. The warranty ensures that we take responsibility for repairing or replacing the faulty product within the designated time frame. Please note that the terms and conditions of the warranty may vary and limitations may apply, depending on the specific circumstances or conditions of product usage.
Safeway Tailgate Co., Ltd. offers Onsite Service to provide off-site assistance for our customers, ensuring convenience and prompt resolution for any operational issues they may encounter. Our Onsite Service allows customers to receive efficient support and troubleshooting directly at their location.
We provide a free car inspection service to our customers. However, please note that this service is available only at our company, Safeway Tailgate Co., Ltd. Customers are required to bring their vehicles to our designated location for the inspection.
We provide a free service of lubricant oil change and grease throughout the lifespan of your vehicle, aiming to enhance performance for the benefit of our customers.
